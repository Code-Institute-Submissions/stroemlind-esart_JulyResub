All the posters are handmade on acrylic paper, drawn with pen and different types of markers. The process of creating the posters is both digital and handmade. First, the motive picture gets edited to make the motive fit the desired look. When done with the editing process, the artist starts to draw the motive on acrylic paper with a pen. With the primary line drawn, the process of coloring the poster begins. The artist sometimes uses colored pencils depending on the poster and motive, but primarily black markers in different sizes for most posters.
